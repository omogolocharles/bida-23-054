Extra

The second-longest-running entertainment magazine on television, EXTRA puts a hip, fresh spin on the entertainment news genre. EXTRA is a leader in innovation and original content, combining a number of interactive and social networking elements with technological advances to bring viewers up-to-the-minute entertainment news, red carpet access, style and fashion news, hot trends and more. Through its Lifechangers segment, EXTRA has also become entertainment television's ultimate headquarters for health and wellness, offering the best experts in the fields of medicine, prevention, wellness, nutrition and fitness.
